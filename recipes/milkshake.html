Milkshakes

"What makes a great summer? A tall glass of milkshake"

Enjoy your summer pool days with a chilling glass of milkshake on the side.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large scoops of ice-cream of your choice
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Whipped cream, for garnish
  • Sprinkles, for garnish
  • Maraschino cherry, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.
  2. Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
